HTML5:

HTML5 is a coding language where you design websites. In this part of web design, you do the words and a little bit of the organization. Another helpful thing to know is that all developers need to know HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.


CSS3:

CSS3 is the visual part of web development, or where you design your website. In CSS3, you can change the color of your website's backround and where you can move text and change its color.


JavaScript:

Javascript is the third part of web development. JavaScript is where you make the interactive parts of websites like buttons and links.


Backend:

Backend is the part of a website that users don't see. It is the part of a website that stores data or calculates it. Backend is also what makes front-end development possible.
